Anno IVxvii e.v. · March 20 – April 10, 2026

The High Holy Season

From the Equinox of the Gods to the last Day of the Receiving

Each day of the High Holy Season carries its own Hebrew letter, Tarot trump, and elemental or planetary attribution drawn from the traditional Thelemic system. The prescribed reading for each day follows the assigned Liber and chapter. March 20 carries two distinct workings — a morning rite (TAV · Earth) and an evening rite (TAV · Saturn). The season closes on April 10 with ALEPH, The Fool — the last letter becomes the first, and the cycle begins again.

🕯 Morning Ritual
Perform the Star Ruby at sunrise. Then perform or solemnly recite Liber V vel Reguli — the Supreme Ritual of the New Aeon. Light a single white candle on a bare altar. The year begins in Earth.

At the exact Equinox moment (14:46 UTC — adjust to local time), pause for 5–10 minutes of silent meditation. Then feast: wine, food, music. Perform Liber Resh at sunset. Let the evening be joyful.
